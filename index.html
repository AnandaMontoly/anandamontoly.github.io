About Me

Hello, I'm Ananda and I'm a junior at Smith College in Northampton Massachusetts. I'm majoring in computer science and I have an as-of-yet undeclared minor in mathematics with a focus in statistics. I am interested in working as a data scientist or as a software engineer after graduation. I'm also interested in getting either a masters degree or a Ph.D. I love diving into a piece of code and solving problems. The process of untangling an error and finding exactly where the issue arises is one which I deeply enjoy. Writing guides to tech and computer science is something else I deeply enjoy as well.

In my free time, I am an avid knitter, crafter, and reader. My favorite book is The Left Hand of Darkness by Ursula K. LeGuin.
